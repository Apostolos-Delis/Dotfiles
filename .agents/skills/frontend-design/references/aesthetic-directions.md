# Aesthetic Directions

Starting points for design tone. Pick one extreme and commit:

- **Brutally minimal** — stripped to essence, every pixel earns its place
- **Maximalist chaos** — dense, layered, overwhelming in a good way
- **Retro-futuristic** — CRT glow, scan lines, tomorrow as imagined yesterday
- **Organic/natural** — flowing shapes, earth tones, living textures
- **Luxury/refined** — restrained elegance, premium materials feel
- **Playful/toy-like** — rounded, bouncy, candy-colored, delightful
- **Editorial/magazine** — strong grid, dramatic type hierarchy, editorial photography feel
- **Brutalist/raw** — exposed structure, monospace, intentionally rough
- **Art deco/geometric** — sharp angles, gold accents, symmetrical patterns
- **Soft/pastel** — gentle gradients, muted tones, dreamy atmosphere
- **Industrial/utilitarian** — function-first, steel and concrete feel, no decoration

Use these for inspiration but design something true to the specific context.
